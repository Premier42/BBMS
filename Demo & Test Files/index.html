Blood Donation Process

Knowing about blood donation can help dispel myths and encourage others to donate:

  • Types of blood donations (whole blood, plasma, platelets)
  • Eligibility criteria for donors
  • Importance of regular donations

Educating yourself about blood donation can help you make informed decisions and save lives.

Learn more about the blood donation process, eligibility criteria, and frequency:

  • Who can donate: Individuals meeting specific age, weight, and health requirements.
  • How often: Guidelines for donation frequency based on the type of donation (whole blood, plasma, platelets).
  • Health benefits: The impact of regular blood donation on community health and well-being.
